Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Assist AI handles information on assistia.digital and the ReviewPro application. It is written in clear language so users can understand the main principles.

Information you provide

When you use ReviewPro, you may enter a business name, activity type and city. You may also contact us by email. Do not enter confidential personal information or private customer data into public search fields.

Information processed by services

ReviewPro uses configured third-party services to search public place information and generate reports. These services may process the information needed to complete the request. Assist AI aims to use only the information necessary for the function of the tool.

Website analytics and advertising

The website may use analytics or advertising technologies to understand site usage and support the service. Advertising code is intended for content-rich pages, not blank pages, contact screens or tool screens without publisher content.

Email contact

If you contact us by email, we will use your email address and message to respond. We do not ask users to send sensitive personal information.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the website and application evolve. The latest version will be available on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@assistia.digital.
