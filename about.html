About Assist AI

Assist AI creates practical tools that help people understand information faster and make clearer decisions. ReviewPro is the first public tool in this direction, focused on reputation audits and local business insight.

Our purpose

Many owners and managers know that online reputation matters, but they do not always have time to read reviews, compare competitors and turn feedback into an action plan. Assist AI aims to make that work more structured. The goal is not to replace the manager. The goal is to make useful signals easier to see.

What ReviewPro does

ReviewPro combines a business search, Google rating signals, review volume, available review text and local benchmark comparison. It then generates a report with a summary, strengths, weaknesses, issues to monitor, concrete recommendations and a thirty-day action plan.

Editorial approach

The guides on this website are written to explain the concepts behind the tool. They are practical, educational and focused on responsible interpretation. We avoid presenting automated scores as absolute truth. Public reviews are useful but incomplete, so every report should be combined with local knowledge and human judgement.
